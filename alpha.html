Machine Learning models for Predicting and Preventing Customer Churn

March, 2018 - August, 2018

Winning a customer is only one half of the task for a company. It’s also about engaging, and ultimately retaining, customers to achieve long-term success. Solving the issue of customer churn has been one of the biggest challenges, both for brands and the product teams that develop the online user experience. The more we can forecast churn, the better we can prevent it. With machine learning models, we can understand what’s specifically causing churn. Product managers, developers, designers, and executives are spared the guessing games.

Knowledge Gained

  1. Determining different aspects related to a company that might cause churning data.
  2. Development of predictive probabilistic Machine Learning Models.
  3. Drawing inferences from results in order to change the flawed aspects for preventing future churns.
  4. Developing Interactive Visualisation Techniques.
