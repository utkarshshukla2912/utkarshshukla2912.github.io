Deep Auto-encoders Models and Visualisation Techniques for Analysis Of Student Feedbacks

July, 2018 - December, 2018

The Goal is to build Deep Learning models for analysis of student feedbacks for extracting actionable content related to courses and professors, also devising a statistical Parser for determining complaints and suggestions from feedbacks. The second aspect focuses on providing simple insightful visualization over dashboards for quick inferences and actions.

Knowledge Gained

  • Preprocessing involved in textual data.
  • Different word embeddings and text representation techniques.
  • Applying Deep learning models over a high dimensional feature vector.
  • Developing Interactive Visualisation Techniques.
  • Integration of WebApps with Deep Learning Framework for real-time Predictions
